Site Name: Ozamiz City Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Ozamiz City Chamber of Commerce and Industry Foundation, Inc. is a local organization of businesses and companies in Ozamiz City with the intention to develop and further the interests of local companies and businesses in the city

Chambers of Commerce main activities are, among others, safeguarding business interests and sharing business experiences and business interests, contact with governments, civil society, local media and the press and organzing trade shows and events.
