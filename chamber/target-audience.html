Persona 1

Food Entrepreneur Cathy

Cathy has just started her own business. Her main business product is homemade Catfish Chips. As a newbie, Cathy has no information about good catfish supplier, and it is impossible for her to farm catfish by herself, as she lives in the city. She has to buy the catfish from the "middle-man", which is quite expensive and sometimes the middle man will ran out of stock as he also supplies catfish to other businesses.

Demographics

  • Age:32
  • Gender:Female
  • Job:Entrepreneur
  • Location:Ozamiz City
  • Education:Bachelor's Degree
  • Income:Php 50k to 80k

Goals and Motivations

Find a catfish farmer to supply fresh and high quality catfish instead of buying from a middle man

Expand the business and open another restaurant in neighboring towns and cities

Frustrations

Cannot increase or expand the business because of the limited supply of raw materials

Persona 2

Catfish Farmer Ben

Catfish farmer Ben owns a large catfish farm in a small village at the outskirts of Ozamiz City. He always wanted to increas his revenue from catfish farming but since he lives far away from the center of commerce it's hard for him to sell catfish in large quantities. All of his regular customers usually are villagers who buy catfish in small quantities on a daily basis.

Demographics

  • Age:50
  • Gender:Male
  • Job:Farmer
  • Location:Ozamiz City
  • Education:None
  • Income:Php 30k

Goals and Motivations

Reach out to customers outside his village and supply catfish to food entrepreneur and restaurant owners

Expand his catfish farm and add 10 more ponds to increase production

Frustrations

Cannot increase or expand the business because of the limited number of consumers buying catfish

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a direct supplier of fresh catfish?
  • Anyone interested to buy high quality catfish on a daily basis?
  • I wanted to expand my business, where can I borrow the needed capital?
  • I wanted to give back to the community, are there any events that I can join?
  • I wanted to get to know more about the people in my area. Where can I go?
